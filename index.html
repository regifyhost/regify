System Info

Is there a free version of Regify?
No. Regify is a professional-grade tool. We offer a 1-Hour Full Pro Access Trial so you can experience real gains before committing to a license.
Does the license key expire?
The $15 license is a one-time purchase. Once activated on your HWID, you have lifetime access to Regify and all future updates within the V5.x–V6.x version series.
Is Regify safe for competitive games?
Yes. Regify only modifies Windows Registry parameters and system settings. We do not inject into game processes, making it safe for EAC, BattlEye, and Vanguard.
What is the Regify Anti-Malware system?
Introduced in V5.1, Regify Anti-Malware is a new protection layer built into the core. It prevents cracking attempts, blocks reverse engineering, and detects unauthorized modifications to ensure your installation is always legitimate and secure.
What changed from beta to full release?
V5.1 Full includes a completely redesigned UI, additional performance optimizations based on beta feedback, and the all-new Regify Anti-Malware protection layer. The full release is a significant upgrade over the beta in every aspect.
